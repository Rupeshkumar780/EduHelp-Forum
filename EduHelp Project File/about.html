ABOUT

A website aimed at making studies more interactive by building a community among college batchmates and seniors can be a valuable tool for students.

Features and Functionality

  • Student Registration: Enable students to register with their email addresses to verify their identity.
  • Resource Sharing: Enable users to share study materials, notes, and resources.
  • Doubt Section: Students can post their doubts which can be answered by peers or seniors.
